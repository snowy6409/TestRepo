Live-action commercials and content
With more than two decades of experience, Quad Productions has a long reputation of high production-value and highly creative live action storytelling. Quad fosters an international roster of directors that are dedicated to telling beautiful stories in any genre and create groundbreaking content for the biggest international agencies, brands and creative partners.

Live-action and animated feature films
Since 2004 Quad has opened its activity to producing feature films. This department now boasts 14 films, including international hits HEARTBREAKER and THE INTOUCHABLES (box-office record for non-English-language film with 51 million tickets sold worldwide). Now one of the most influential companies on the French market, Quad produces quality entertainment with strong concepts via contemporary and relatable themes. Today its universal approach to story-telling targets both French moviegoers and international audiences.

100% food
